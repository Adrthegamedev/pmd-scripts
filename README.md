# pmd-scripts
Various scripts we have made to mess around with Pokémon Mystery Dungeon's internal data files.

pmdwiiextract.py extracts the "data1.bin" and "data2.bin" from the wiiware Pokémon Mystery Dungeon games, and "pmdsmexaminer.py" & "pmdswexaminer.py" flip the .smd and .swd files endianness from "smdb" and "swdb" to "smdl" and "swdl" and vice versa.
